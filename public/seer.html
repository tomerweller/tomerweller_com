SEER

Predictive speed dialer

A speed dialer application for Android. Seer predicts your next call based on a patented machine learning algorithm which takes into account your call log history and your current context - time and location.

The SEER algorithm was patented in the Hebrew University by Assif Ziv and Ohad Assoulin in 2011. In 2013 I was invited by Assif to resurrect Seer and turn it into a viable commercial app. I brought my expertise in modern mobile applications and agile development.

Seer is currently in wide scale public beta. It's supported by "Yissom", the Hebrew University's technology transfer company
